# Module 01 - Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC)

## Overview

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) in Cybersecurity. It covers the principles, standards and frameworks that organizations use to establish, implement and continuously improve an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

This documentation is part of my **Cybersecurity Journey 2026–2027**, where I document my learning process through notes, labs and practical projects while transitioning into the cybersecurity field.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the fundamentals of Governance in Cybersecurity.
- Learn how an Information Security Management System (ISMS) works.
- Understand the relationship between Risk Management and Information Security.
- Learn the purpose of international standards and cybersecurity frameworks.
- Build a solid foundation before studying defensive and offensive security.

---

## Topics Covered

- Governance
- Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- ISO/IEC 27001
- ISO/IEC 27005
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- Risk Management
- Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)
- GDPR
- NIS2
- PCI DSS
