# Cybersecurity Glossary

---

## Information Security

**Definition**

Information Security is the discipline responsible for protecting information in any format, including physical, digital, or verbal, ensuring its confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

**Why is it important?**

Organizations rely on information to operate. Protecting that information helps prevent financial losses, legal issues, and reputational damage.

**Example**

A printed contract, a database, and a confidential meeting are all assets protected by Information Security.

---

## Cybersecurity

**Definition**

Cybersecurity is a branch of Information Security focused on protecting digital systems, networks, applications, devices, and electronic information against cyber threats.

**Why is it important?**

Cybersecurity helps organizations reduce the risk of cyberattacks, malware, ransomware, data breaches, and unauthorized access.

**Example**

A firewall, antivirus software, multi-factor authentication, and endpoint protection are cybersecurity controls.
